    1. Connect Dashboard (Offer-Centric)¶

    The primary dashboard shows aggregate data on my Offers and inbound Offers.
    It surfaces both state counts and actionable prompts.

    Lifecycle Flow¶

    • Drafts ‚Äî Offers not yet placed in a Space (parked, not visible).
    • Active Offers ‚Äî visible in a Space, subdivided by matching state:
      • Under-constrained ‚Üí too many matches; suggests requirements to add.
      • Over-constrained ‚Üí too few/none; suggests requirements to relax.
      • Waiting for My Responses ‚Üí unknowns I must answer.
      • Waiting on Others ‚Üí unknowns others must answer.
      • Healthy Fit ‚Üí matches in desired range but below Agreement threshold.
    • Agreement Phase ‚Üí matches ‚â• Agreement Phase Threshold; ready to reveal identity and negotiate.
    • Agreed / Closed ‚Üí historical Offers (archived for reference).

    Dashboard Interactions¶

    • Click a header (e.g. ‚ÄúOver-constrained‚Äù):
      • Reveals Top 3 prompts relevant to that state.
        • For ‚ÄúOver-constrained‚Äù: top 3 requirements to consider relaxing.
        • For ‚ÄúWaiting for My Responses‚Äù: top missing promises I‚Äôm asked to make.
    • Click a number (e.g. ‚Äú7 Over-constrained Offers‚Äù):
      • Opens a Collection View of Offer Cards in that state.
      • From there, I can drill into any card‚Äôs Feedback Panel.

    3. Feedback Panel (Card Drill-Down)¶

    When I drill into a specific Offer Card:

    4. Resonance Engine Dashboard (Promise-Centric)¶

    A complementary dashboard for sense-making into a Space.
    Instead of Offers, it organizes around Promises themselves.

    What it surfaces¶

    • Top Resonant Promises ‚Äî promises most frequently included in offers in this space.
    • Aspirational Gaps ‚Äî promises often required in offers but rarely present in members‚Äô LifeCodes.
    • Cultural Climate Heatmap ‚Äî % of members aligned with key promises (values, principles).
    • Growth Prompts ‚Äî promises I‚Äôm being nudged to consider adding to my LifeCode to unlock more connections.

    Why it matters¶

    • Lets individuals see where they resonate with the culture of a space.
    • Lets spaces see where alignment is strong or thin, and adapt over time.
    • Complements the Offer-centric dashboard by showing the memetic layer of connection.

    5. Design Notes¶

    • States are mutually exclusive and clear: Draft ‚Üí Active (with sub-status) ‚Üí Agreement Phase ‚Üí Closed.
    • Top 3 prompts ensure dashboards surface actionable insights, not just numbers.
    • Cross-cutting pivots: Clicking any Promise or Requirement shows both its definition (link to Meme Pool) and the collection of Offers it affects.
    • Threshold awareness: Agreement Phase Threshold is explicit; exceeding it unlocks negotiation tools.
    • Privacy posture: Spaces see only alignment signals; only I see my private answers.
